Terms:
-A risk assessment is performed to determine the most important potential security breaches to address
	-analyse vulnerabilities
	-which attacks can be successful
-A threat assessment is performed to determine the best approaches for securing a system against a particular threat, or class of threats
	-analyse attacker's resources 
	-which attacks exist

Interruption: an information asset became unusable/inaccessible/lost
Interception: unauthorized party gained access to a resource
Modification: a resource has been modified by an unauthorized party
Fabrication: counterfeiting of an asset
